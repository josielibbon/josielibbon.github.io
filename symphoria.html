Symphoria

For those who may not know, Symphoria is a live professional orchestra and chorus that performs in Syracuse, garnering audiences of around 5,000 people per show. From 2016-2018, I was given the wonderful opportunity to perform in around 10 of their shows. As the orchestra played and the chorus sang, I would perform a combination of ballet, jazz, and modern in front of them, to enhance the audience's experience of the beautiful music being performed. It was truly an honor to perform with these talented artists.
